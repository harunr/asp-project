ESTAVIDA

  • Estavida is an upbeat tapas lounge located in the hotel lobby serving innovative tapas and a wide selection of cocktails.
  • Guests will enjoy light bites during the day or sip on Estavida’s signature Apricot Sangria or Gin “Peel” Tonic while reveling in the lounge’s lively, energetic atmosphere into the late evening hours.
  • Opening Times: 5:00 PM – 1:00 AM
  • Dress Code: Casual
