NIKKEI

  • Celebrated for its exquisite fusion of Japanese, Cantonese and Peruvian cuisine, Nikkei, features a sushi bar and Teppanyaki tables.
  • Menu highlights include the Crunchy Thai Salad, Salmon & Avocado Ceviche, Japanese Chicken Curry and Encrusted Sea Bass with Curry Mango Sauce.
  • Opening Times: 6:00 PM – 10:30 PM; Reservations Required.
  • Dress Code: Dressy Casual
