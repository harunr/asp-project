What better way to spend your first evening in Sicily than with a beautiful barbecue feast on a private beach surrounded by friends and colleagues? Checked tablecloths, flickering lanterns, an attentive wait staff and the Bay of Mazzaro shimmering just beyond the sand create a traditional Sicilian welcome. We invite you to dress casually, bring a sweater or light jacket for cool sea breezes and wear shoes you can walk in on sand.

Dates:
  • Sunday, October 25, 2020 – 7:00pm – 9:00pm
Additional Information:
  • Attire: Casual clothing and comfortable shoes; sweater or light jacket
