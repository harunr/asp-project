Get ready to step back in time to the 1940s and the intrigue of “The Godfather” at Castello degli Schiavi, a rural Baroque castle dating back to 700 AD. The walled villa nestled among the orange groves of the beautiful Riviera di Taormina has a rich past marked by extraordinary events. Legend says Turkish pirates kidnapped the castle’s owners to sell them as slaves, giving it the name, “The Castle of the Slaves.” In 1971 and 1974, it was the setting of some of the main scenes of “The Godfather” and “The Godfather: Part II.” This evening, another extraordinary event takes place here: a gala evening created exclusively for Ricoh Family Group.

The Godfather aura and era will come to life as you enjoy cocktails, an elegant dinner and a live trio performing unforgettable soundtracks from the movie and other classics. Surprise appearances and happenings will have you talking about tonight for years to come!
