Memorable highlights of Sicily’s Old-World beauty, landmarks and culture are in store during this half-day tour. An English-speaking guide will accompany you on a motorcoach drive to Savoca, a breathtaking village set in the rock between two hills; and to Forza d’Agrò, a village with a wonderful medieval atmosphere. Sites along the walking tour in Savoca include the Bar Vitelli, famous for appearing in “The Godfather” movie; San Michele Church; Santa Lucia Church built in the 15th and 16th centuries; and the remains of a Norman castle.
