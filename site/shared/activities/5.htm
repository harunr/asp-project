The hotel’s serene Wellness Centre is set among lush gardens overlooking the sea. Treatments draw on

Sicily’s most fragrant scents, including orange flowers, sandalwood and jasmine, and many products are crafted from pure essential oils from wild plants. You may choose one of these treatment options:

  • Traditional Massage (50 minutes): Enjoy a full body massage designed to ease tension, soothe stress and induce a sense of wellbeing.
  • Muscle Melt Massage (50 minutes): Your therapist drizzles a blend of warm oils over your body and follows with a muscle massage. Intense strokes and flowing techniques promote a calm and relaxed state.
  • Aromatherapy Massage (50 minutes): This harmonizing massage uses essential oils to enhance physical, emotional and mental wellbeing. Tension melts away, leaving you blissfully relaxed.
  • Lymphatic Drainage Massage (50 minutes): Improve your circulation and benefit from a gentle, natural expulsion of toxins and excess fluid from the body.
  • Purifying Facial (50 minutes): Revitalize your skin with this classic, deep cleansing facial that includes steam, face and décolleté massage, face mask and customized cream. It leaves your skin smooth, purified and instantly brighter.
  • Argan Anti-Aging Facial (50 minutes): For complexions in need of a regenerating boost, this treatment nourishes and oxygenates your skin using wild argan oil and concentrated Dolomite-based silica. The experience starts by exfoliating the skin with a re-mineralizing green clay, followed by a soothing face, neck and décolleté massage and a silicic acid gel mask to relax fine lines and wrinkles and fade age spots and signs of sun damage. Finally, argan and silicon cream is applied to complete the deeply reparative and regenerating effect, leaving skin radiant and lustrous.
  • Tamanu Nourishing Facial (50 minutes): Polynesian women have long used tamanu, a tropical nut oil, to keep their skin youthful and healthy. The oil’s precious anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties make this treatment ideal for dry, dehydrated skin affected by the first signs of aging. Your therapist will cleanse your skin and apply a green clay exfoliant, perform a relaxing face, neck and décolleté massage and smooth on nourishing tamanu cream.
  • Persian Rose Calming Facial (50 minutes): In cosmetology, the Persian rose is prized for endowing skin with the same velvety texture as its petals. Enjoy its soothing properties and intoxicating fragrance in this calming facial, ideal for delicate, fragile or inflamed skin. After cleansing your skin and applying an exfoliating Italian green clay, your therapist will gently massage your face, neck and décolleté. Finally, a calming and refreshing cream crafted from beautiful Persian rose essential oil is applied.
