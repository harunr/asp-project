Get to know Taormina in a completely new way! The fun begins when you and your Ricoh colleagues and guests gather in Restaurant Oliviero for a brief orientation and grouping into teams. Ricoh staff will escort you to Piazza IX Aprile and give each team an iPad with a map of Taormina and a list of challenges to complete. As you stop at famous sites throughout the city, you will photograph your team, solve puzzles and answer questions about Taormina. At the end of the hunt, all teams will meet at the fashionable La Giara Restaurant for lunch and to celebrate the winners.

Group orientation in Restaurant Oliviero: 9:00 am

Lunch: 12:00 pm

Dates:
  • Monday, October 26 – 9:30am – 2:00pm
Additional Information:
  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Attire: Comfortable, casual clothing and shoes, hat and sunglasses
