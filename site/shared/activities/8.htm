Villa Britannia is a small, exclusive, boutique B&B located in the center of Taormina, surrounded by Sicilian gardens with views over the sea and the city. Villa Britannia hosts an ancient Roman soldier tomb in its garden and is the neighboring villa to where the famous writers D.H Lawrence and Truman Capote lived.

This charming villa is away from the chaotic traffic of Corso Umberto but very close and within walking distance of the Grand Hotel Timeo. Guests will feel at home but will experience and taste a true side of Sicily.

Your private cooking class will teach you how to make 3 different Sicilian dishes using only what Sicily offers best in season. Each dish is prepared from scratch using locally sourced ingredients. After the class, lunch will be served while you enjoy the fruits of your labor.
