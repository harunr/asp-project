This adventure-filled day starts with a minibus ride from the hotel to GRAVE, where a fleet of 10 off-road buggies awaits. Get behind the wheel for a three-hour drive through the beautiful Alcantara River area punctuated by hazel groves, vineyards and lava flows. In the stretch between Castiglione and Francavilla di Sicilia, you will see a series of lakes with a round shape known as gurne, formed by thousands of years of unceasing erosion on the lava. These waterways are the ideal habitat for aquatic plants such as the rare buttercup brush and other species of great scientific interest. Your tour takes you to the Byzantine Cuba, a small, early Christian chapel dating back to the fifth century BC. Your last stop is at a farmhouse, where you will park your buggy, enjoy lunch and board the minibus for the drive back to the hotel.

An escort car will accompany the buggy caravan.
